Latest Weekly Updates

Trial Result 15 Apr 2026

Beamion LUNG-1 — zongertinib

Lung · Phase I · ORR (BICR) 76%

Advanced/metastatic non-squamous NSCLC with HER2 TKD activating mutations; treatment-naïve; ECOG PS 0-1; 30% brain mets — ORR (BICR): 76% — Zongertinib showed sustained efficacy in previously untreated patients with advanced HER2-mutant NSCLC. Treatment-related adverse events were predominantly low-grade.

Sources: NEJM, ELCC 2026

Conference 20 Apr 2026

CAR-PRISM — ciltacabtagene autoleucel

Haematology · Phase II · MRD negativity 100%

High-risk smoldering MM per 20/2/20 model; excluded if >40% BM plasma cells; ECOG PS 0-1; median age 58 — MRD negativity: 100% — Cilta-cel as primary therapy induced deep and durable responses in high-risk smoldering multiple myeloma, with 100% MRD negativity and no progression to active disease at median 15.3 months follow-up.

Sources: AACR, Nature Medicine

Regulatory 13 Apr 2026

IDeate-Lung01 — ifinatamab deruxtecan

Lung · Phase II · ORR (BICR) 48.2%

Adult patients with ES-SCLC with disease progression on or after platinum-based chemotherapy; ≥1 prior line; measurable disease; ECOG PS 0-1 — ORR (BICR): 48.2% — If approved, ifinatamab deruxtecan would be a first-in-class B7-H3 directed DXd ADC for previously treated ES-SCLC.

Sources: FDA.gov, WCLC 2025

Trial Result 13 Apr 2026

OptimUM-02 — darovasertib + crizotinib

Skin · Phase II/III · PFS (BICR) 37.1% vs 5.8%

First-line HLA-A*02:01-negative metastatic uveal melanoma; age ≥18; ECOG PS 0-1; measurable disease; no prior systemic therapy in metastatic setting — PFS (BICR): 37.1% vs 5.8% — OptimUM-02 is the first randomized study to demonstrate a statistically significant and clinically meaningful benefit in PFS in first-line HLA-A*02:01-negative metastatic uveal melanoma.

Sources: OncLive, Press release

Guideline 14 Apr 2026

ESMO mCRC Guidelines 2026

GI · Precision Oncology

Patients with metastatic colorectal cancer — The 2026 ESMO Clinical Practice Guideline highlights precision oncology in mCRC, with treatment decisions shaped by molecular biology and refined therapeutic sequencing including anti-EGFR rechallenge. Key biomarkers: RAS, BRAF V600E, MMR/MSI, HER2 at diagnosis; ctDNA when tissue unavailable.

Sources: ESMO, Annals of Oncology
