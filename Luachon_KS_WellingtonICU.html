Wellington ICU Antibiotics

Drug Manual - Appendix 5

Clinical Guidance & Definitions

ESCHAPPM² Organisms

Enterobacter, Serratia, Citrobacter freundii, Hafnia, Acinetobacter/Aeromonas, Proteus (not mirabilis), Providencia & Morganella morganii.

ESBL Rules

ESBL-producing organisms are resistant to most beta-lactams. Carbapenems are the agent of choice.

C. difficile Protocol

C. difficile should ONLY be treated with Metronidazole or Vancomycin (Oral).
